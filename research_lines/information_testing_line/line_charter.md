# information_testing_research_line
# polyarb_lab — research_line (active)

## Purpose

Convert raw information items into codified, testable, comparable research outputs.

This line is NOT for:
- claiming profitability
- contaminating mainline
- promoting ideas without evidence
- keeping vague ideas unstructured

## Isolation rule

No output from this line enters mainline without:
1. Repeated research value demonstrated
2. Predictive lift, improved observation quality, or better executable sample relevance shown
3. Explicit promotion decision logged in line notes

## Evaluation standard

Judge this line by:
- Whether information becomes testable
- Whether understanding improves
- Whether ranking/scoring/observation quality improves
- Whether higher-information samples are revealed
- Whether future upgrade candidates are created

Do NOT judge by:
- Immediate profitability
- Raw candidate count alone
- Excitement or novelty alone

## Item lifecycle

collect → classify → codify → test (paper/research only) → store output → judge

Final classification options:
- keep: active research value, continue testing
- downgrade: low value but not zero; reduce priority
- reject: no evidence of value; stop
- park: indeterminate; revisit when conditions change
- escalate: repeated research value, promotion candidate

## Evidence standard

- An idea is NOT validated unless evidence clearly supports it
- More raw output is NOT progress unless information value improves
- Vague ideas that cannot be codified within one iteration → reject or park

## Categories supported

- external_script_tool_idea
